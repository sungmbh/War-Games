üåê Program Overview & Goals

The CES2026 Deal Sprint is an 8-hour, high-intensity simulation designed to teach Taiwan startups the core principles needed for success at the Consumer Electronics Show.

KEY LEARNING OBJECTIVES

  • Lead Generation: Build a usable 20-person CES target list (Research Phase).
  • Selling Principles: Practice pitching to 7 distinct buyer personas (Gate 1).
  • Deal Negotiation: Negotiate commercial terms under pressure (Gate 3).

CORE DYNAMICS

  • Scarcity: Only 18 Gate 1 slots for 20 teams (Auction Format).
  • Recovery Paths: The Hot Lead Surprise and Intel Station ensure that all 20 teams remain engaged and have a path to earning cash.

‚úÖ Program Assessment (First-Time Execution)

STRENGTHS (Built-in Success Factors)

  • Low Tracking Overhead: Kyle only tracks 3 official checkpoints (CP1, CP2, Final Count).
  • Robust Structure: The "Pitch $\rightarrow$ Proof $\rightarrow$ Negotiate" progression is a highly effective pedagogical design.
  • Mentor Clarity: Persona cards give mentors clear, scripted decision criteria.

HIGH RISKS (Recommendations for Staff)

  • Timing Collapse: Volker's MC role is critical. Must use a loud timer and rigidly call "Time's Up" for all meetings/waves.
  • Mentor Compliance: The "TEXT KYLE IMMEDIATELY" step must be practiced by all 7 buyers to keep the leaderboard accurate.
  • Staff Practice: Run a 10-minute dry run of the Gate 1 $\rightarrow$ Checkpoint 1 cash/text flow the day before.

ü§ù Meeting Mechanics & Booking Process

A detailed breakdown of how each set of meetings is secured.

Gate 1: Auction Booking (11:30 AM)

Method: Email Auction (First-come + Quality = Success). Capacity: Only 18 slots total.

  • Process: Teams email their chosen buyer with a bid ($\$2k-\$5k$).
  • Buyer Action: Buyers check emails every 5 min. They select the highest quality/most relevant bids.

Gate 2 & 3: Physical Booking (1:30 PM Break)

Gate 2 Booking (1:30 PM)

Teams who **Passed Gate 1** physically book their 12-min time slot at the Buyer Station on a First-Come, First-Served basis (approx 3-5 meetings/buyer).

Gate 3 Booking (1:30 PM)

Teams who **Passed Gate 2** physically book their 15-min time slot. Capacity is limited (approx 3 meetings/buyer).

Recovery Path Booking

  • HOT LEAD (2:30 PM): All 20 teams compete via email for **one** bonus Gate 2 slot. Winner gets the meeting and the **$5,000 bonus**.
  • INTEL STATION (2:00 PM): Idle teams walk over, post name on "Available Now" board, and wait for a free buyer to come for a session.

‚öôÔ∏è Mechanics & Risk Mitigation (Staff Reference)

STAFF MONEY MECHANICS (Kyle/Jade)

  • Kyle's Task: Tracks digitally only at 3 checkpoints.
  • Jade's Role: Collects cash payments for gate entry (puts in locked box).
  • Tracking Simplification: Buyers pay out from pre-counted stacks (eliminates counting burden).

CRITICAL RISK MITIGATION

PROBLEM: Mentor Text Delay

**SOLUTION:** Volker reminds buyers during breaks. Kyle can ask buyers directly and update retroactively.

PROBLEM: Lost Cash

**SOLUTION:** Team's responsibility. No reimbursement (Game design feature). Kyle's tracker is the final truth for disputes.
