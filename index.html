Program Overview

Goal: Finish with the most cash by 4:00 PM. The scoreboard is money. Morning = email auction; afternoon = diligence + negotiation; recovery via Intel, Hot Lead, War Room.

Scarcity: Exactly 18 Gate-1 meetings total. Exactly 18 Gate-2 total (max 3 per mentor). ≤7 Gate-3 total (max 1 per mentor).
PhaseWhat Happens
Research (10:00)Build a 20-person CES target list. Optional War Room prep (US$1,000).
Gate-1 Auction → DiscoveryEach team sends ONE cold email to ONE mentor at 11:30 with a bid (US$2k–US$5k, in 1k). Mentor selects up to cap on fit first, bid as tie-breaker. Payout: US$3,000 if “Interested,” else US$0. Overflow Draw fills remaining slots; two teams end with no slot → out of Gate-1.
Gate-2 Qualification12-min Q&A with same mentor. Entry fee: US$2,000. Payout: US$8,000 / US$5,000 / US$0 (Strong/Decent/Weak). 18 total across mentors.
Gate-3 Negotiation15-min deal discussion. Entry fee: US$3,000. Payout: US$15,000 / US$10,000 / US$0 (Deal/Conditional/No Deal). ≤7 total, max 1 per mentor.
Intel Station (2:00–3:45)8-min buyer consults. Payouts: US$0 / US$1,000 / US$2,000 (mentor-set value).
Hot Lead (2:30)5-minute blitz. Winner earns an instant US$5,000 bonus + a meeting slot.
