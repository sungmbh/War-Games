The CES Deal Sprint simulates the high-pressure environment of CES, where 20 Taiwan startups compete to win deals from 6 corporate buyers. It's a war game designed to prepare you for real CES 2026.

🚪 The 3 Gates Explained

GATE 1
Discovery
⏱️ Time: 5 minutes
💸 Cost: $2,000
🔑 Need: Nothing
💰 Win: $3,000 + 🔑 Key
📋 Format: First-come booking

Goal: Prove you're relevant to the buyer's mandate. Quick pitch showing you understand their role and needs.

Tips: Research buyer. Be concise. Show specific relevance.

GATE 2
Qualification
⏱️ Time: 12 minutes
💸 Cost: $2,000
🔑 Need: Gate 1 Key
💰 Win: $5-8k + 🔑🔑 Master Key
📋 Format: 5 fixed waves

Goal: Answer buyer's role-specific challenge. Show depth, substance, and technical understanding.

Tips: Prepare deep answers. Know your metrics. Handle tough questions.

GATE 3
Deal Closing
⏱️ Time: 15 minutes
💸 Cost: $3,000
🔑 Need: Master Key + Partner Token
💰 Win: $10-15k
📋 Format: 3 fixed slots

Goal: Present implementation plan. Negotiate terms. Handle objections. Close the final deal.

Tips: Have concrete 90-day plan. Prepare for objections. Show execution capability.

🔑 The Key System

🔑 Gate 1 Key
Physical token from passing Gate 1. Required to enter Gate 2. Can be bought/sold in Key Market during lunch!
🔑🔑 Master Key
Special token from passing Gate 2. Required to enter Gate 3. Shows you've proven substance.
🤝 Partner Token
Signed endorsement from another startup. Required for Gate 3. Build partnerships throughout the day!

🚀 Startup Strategy Guide

🎯 Winning Strategy

Winners aren't just the best startups - they're teams with the best strategy: choosing right buyers, managing capital wisely, executing under pressure.

💡 Key Strategic Decisions

Which Buyer to Target?

Don't pitch everyone! Match your solution to buyer's mandate. Perfect-fit pitch to right buyer beats generic pitch to multiple buyers.

When to Use Ken?

If you have <$12k after leads: Consider Ken ($1k) to pick right buyer. If you have $15k+: Skip Ken, book multiple buyers directly.

Capital Management?

Conservative: 1 Gate 1 + 1 Gate 2 + 1 Gate 3 = $34k final
Aggressive: 2 Gate 1 + 2 Gate 2 + 2 Gate 3 = $45k+ final

Partner Token Hunt?

Start during Gate 1! Don't wait until Gate 3. Need signed endorsement from another startup. Network throughout the day.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Pitching wrong buyer - Healthcare startup pitching automotive = instant fail
  • Spending too fast - Booking 5 Gate 1 meetings = $10k spent, no capital left
  • Not finding partner - Need Partner Token for Gate 3, start hunting early!
  • Generic pitches - "Our AI platform..." = boring. "We solve YOUR problem X" = wins
  • Ignoring feedback - Buyer says "not ready"? Listen, learn, apply to next pitch

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Research wins - 30 min researching buyer = 10× better pitch
  • Speed matters - First-mover advantage in Gate 1 booking
  • Partner early - Don't wait until 2 PM to find partner
  • Stay flexible - Market Shift forces adaptation at 1:45
  • Learn fast - Failed Gate 1? Adjust for Gate 2
  • Have fun - This is practice! Experiment, take risks
