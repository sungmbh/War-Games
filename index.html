🚀 Mission & Core Design (V2.2)

To simulate a complete, high-pressure CES sales cycle in one day, maximizing learning and minimizing frustration. Startups compete for scarce meeting slots, forcing quality execution, while ensuring all participants remain engaged and receive value.

Core Design Philosophy (V2.2)

Real Research, Real Tools

Build actual CES target lists using tactics from a 15-min lesson on LinkedIn Sales Navigator & Apollo.io.

Ranked Choice Booking

Teams rank Top 3 mentors. Rewards research over luck and reduces binary failure.

Mathematical Fairness

A transparent scoring rubric where every point is defendable. No subjectivity.

Zero Idle Time (Parallel Ops)

Booked teams run "Partnership Bingo." Unbooked teams enter a 90-min "Workshop Track."

No Public Rejection

Results are delivered privately via email. "Workshop Track" is framed as a positive coaching path.

Learning Encouraged

War Room is a -5 point "Strategic Investment," not a severe penalty, and includes advocacy.

🚀 Your Mission Briefing

Welcome, Startup Teams! Your objective today is to navigate the CES sales battlefield, secure meetings, refine your pitch, and build strategic alliances. Here are your key tasks:

  • Absorb the Sales Tech Lesson (10:00 AM)

    Pay close attention to Josh Fu's 15-minute crash course on using LinkedIn Sales Navigator & Apollo.io. You'll need these skills immediately.

  • Build Your CES Target List (10:15 AM)

    Create a list of 20 real people you want to meet at CES 2026 using the tools and techniques just learned. This is a usable output for your actual CES prep!

  • Craft & Send Your Booking Email (10:45 AM - 12:00 PM)

    Research the mentors (their real backgrounds!). Draft ONE highly personalized email targeting your #1 choice. **Crucially, list your #2 and #3 ranked choices** in the email body. Aim for 15/15 on the rubric (Personalization, Relevance, Clarity). Send it between 11:30 AM and 12:00 PM sharp!

  • Engage in the Speed Networking Challenge (12:00 PM)

    While mentors score emails, practice your 60-second pitch with fellow teams. Use this active waiting time productively.

  • Execute Your Afternoon Mission (13:00 PM)

    Based on your private email result at 12:45 PM:

    • If Booked (16 Teams): Attend your 30-min meeting, complete the "Partnership Bingo" challenge (+15 pts), and find a "Floating Mentor" for feedback on your target list.
    • If Workshop Track (4 Teams): Attend the intensive 90-min coaching session with Ken Huang (13:00-14:30), then prepare your killer 4-minute pitch for the finale.

  • Deliver/Judge in the Finale (15:30 PM)

    Showcase what you've learned:

    • Workshop Track Teams: Give your polished 4-minute pitch on the main stage.
    • Booked Teams: Listen critically and vote for the "Peer's Choice Award" winner (+20 pts).

🎯 The Transparent Scoring System (V2.2)

Critical Design Principle

Every point is mathematically defensible. At the awards ceremony, we will display the champion's complete score breakdown. No subjectivity. Pure merit.

Phase 1: Booking Score

0-15 Points

  • Personalization (0-5)
  • Relevance (0-5)
  • Clarity (0-5)

Phase 1: Booking Success

+30 Points

Awarded to the Top 16 scores via Ranked Choice Booking.

Phase 2: Meeting Score

0-25 Points

  • Research Quality (0-5)
  • Proof Points (0-5)
  • Pitch Structure (0-5)
  • Objection Handling (0-5)
  • Pilot Plan Specificity (0-5)

Phase 2: Paid Pilot (Formula)

The Meeting Score determines the pilot bonus:

  • Score 20-25 = YES (+50 Pts)
  • Score 15-19 = MAYBE (+25 Pts)
  • Score 0-14 = NO (0 Pts)

Phase 3: Partnership Bingo

+15 Points

For submitting a completed "Partnership Bingo Challenge" card.

Phase 3: Peer's Choice Award

+20 Points

Awarded to the Workshop Track startup that wins the audience vote.

Phase 4: War Room Penalty

-5 Points

"Strategic Investment" to consult with Ken. Includes "2nd Look Bonus."

Phase 4: Behavioral Penalties

-5 to -20

  • Spam email (-15)
  • No-show to meeting (-20)
  • Late arrival 5+ min (-10)
  • Unprofessional behavior (-5)

🏆 Complete Scoring Formula (V2.2)

TOTAL SCORE =
  Booking_Score (0-15)
  + Booking_Success (0 or +30)
  + Meeting_Score (0-25)
  + Paid_Pilot_Formula (0, +25, or +50)
  + Partnership_Bingo_Bonus (0 or +15)
  + Peer's_Choice_Award (0 or +20)
  - War_Room_Penalties (-5)
  - Behavioral_Penalties (0 to -40)
